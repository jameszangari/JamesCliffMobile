Biography

James Cliff is a professional in the music industry with more than 7 years of experience beginning with the creation of his DJ business known as "Velocity Music DJ Company". James learned how to mix tracks by using professional software such as Serato and VirtualDJ, which acted as training wheels for his transition to producing his own tracks using some of the most popular Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs), such as Apple's Logic Pro, Avid's Pro Tools, and Ableton's Live Suite (James's preferred DAW). Cliff's career as a DJ provided him with the necessary tools to be able to produce his own tracks on a professional level.

James is currently a sophomore studying Interactive Digital Media at the Westphal College of Media Arts and Design at Drexel University in Philadelphia, PA. While he is focused on becoming a UI/UX Designer, his free time is spent focusing on his other forms of media. With a history of music production, graphic design, and web design, he has taken advantage of the opportunity to combine his interests and become a creator of multiple forms of media.
