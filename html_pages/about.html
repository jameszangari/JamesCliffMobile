Biography

James Cliff has been a professional in the music industry for 5 years with his roots being grounded around Electronic Dance Music. He has had experience producing with some of the most popular Digital Audio Workstations, and currently prides his use of Ableton Live Suite. While James is currently studying Interactive Digital Media at Drexel University in Philadelphia, Cliff is spending his free time focusing on his other forms of media. With a history of music production, graphic design, and web design, he has taken advantage of the opportunity to combine his interests and become a creator of multiple forms of media.
