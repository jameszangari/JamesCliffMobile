Biography

Hey! My name is James and I am currently a sophomore studying Interactive Digital Media at the Westphal College of Media Arts and Design at Drexel University in Philadelphia, PA. I am working towards becoming a UI/UX Designer, while also focusing on other forms of media including music production, graphic design, and web design.

In 2012, I launched my own disc-jockey company, "Velocity Music DJ Company," and I learned how to mix tracks by using professional software such as Serato and VirtualDJ. These audio mixing applications, often referred to as DJ software, acted as training wheels for my transition to music production. I have had experience using some of the most popular Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs), such as Apple's Logic Pro, Avid's Pro Tools, and Ableton's Live Suite, which I currently use.

HZRD

When I first began producing my own music, I went under the alias of "HZRD." My earliest tracks were Electronic Dance Music (EDM). Over the course of 5 years, I produced over 100 projects and officially released 44 of them publicly on SoundCloud.

James Cliff

Currently, I use the name James Cliff as my creative artist portfolio: a collection of my music production, graphic design, web design, and other related digital media.

I am also part of a collective group of artists called "Untitled Recordings" with Cardinal Sin, Catullus, Locke., and Nahom. Click our logo below to visit the website.
