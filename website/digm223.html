HomeGlow


HomeGlow is a concept for home office lighting solutions with the goal to meet the needs of someone who works from home.


Research included: environmentally-friendly and energy efficent lighting options; glare on laptop screens, monitors, and cellphones; stress-inducing environments; clean-air options; and natural colors for lighting.


Per job description and the requirements throughout the day, the objective was to create a lighting product that follows the industrial and office tasks’ recommended illumination levels, while being environmentally-friendly, energy efficent,and providing artifical natural light.
