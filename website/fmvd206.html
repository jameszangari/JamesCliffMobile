Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri

For this project, we were tasked with replacing all of the audio (dialogue, soundtrack, foley, and ADR) of a snipped from the film "Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri." All dialogue was re-recorded "imitating" ADR techniques as well as all foley sounds and effects.

Burn-E
