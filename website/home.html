ABOUT ME

My name is James and I am currently majoring in Interactive Digital Media at the Westphal College of Media Arts and Design at Drexel University in Philadelphia, PA. I am working towards a career in user interfaces and web design, while also focusing on other forms of media including photography, music production / audio mixing, and graphic design.

A portion of my portfolio includes digital media work such as website development, social media management, as well as logo design and branding for two brands: "James Cliff" and "Untitled Recordings," a brand that represents a collective group of underground artists in Philadelphia. We work with local musicians and provide them with placement opportunities on instrumental tracks, small management, and mixing/mastering services.

The James Cliff and Untitled Recordings websites were coded with basic languages: HTML, CSS, & Javascript. They are are both under management by myself to keep content up to date. Below contains portions of my portfolio: Music, Digital Media, Short Films, and Photography.
